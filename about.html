About Us

Shree Panchayat Secondary School is a community-based educational institution located in Pathari, Morang, Nepal. It is affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB) and offers educational programs from Early Childhood Development (ECD) to Grade 10, as well as Plus Two (+2) programs in Management, Science, Education, and Humanities. The school provides a moderate fee structure and offers scholarships to deserving students. It features various facilities such as a cafeteria, library, science and computer labs, and sports facilities. Admission to the school is based on an entrance examination or SEE grades, followed by a personal interview. The school also engages students in co-curricular activities like debating, elocution, and excursions, making it a well-rounded educational environment.

Facilities

Shree Panchayat Secondary School in Pathari, Morang, is well-equipped with a variety of facilities that support both academic and extracurricular activities. The school has a cafeteria offering freshly prepared meals, a well-stocked library with textbooks and reference materials, and fully equipped science and computer labs. It also promotes physical fitness through its sports facilities and provides counseling services for mental health and academic guidance. Additionally, multimedia resources are used to enhance the learning experience, while music and dance facilities foster cultural development. The school offers transportation services and encourages students' participation in co-curricular activities such as debating, essay writing, drama, and excursions. These offerings aim to provide a holistic education to students.

Buildings

Shree Panchayat Secondary School in Pathari, Morang, is equipped with well-structured facilities to cater to the diverse needs of its students. The school boasts two English medium buildings, providing a modern and conducive learning environment for students pursuing English-based education. Additionally, there is a Nepali medium building for students who prefer to learn in their native language. The school also has a dedicated Science medium building, fully equipped with science labs and resources for students in the Science stream. For those in the Humanities stream, there is an Arts building that supports their academic and creative pursuits. This diverse infrastructure ensures that students have access to specialized facilities, making their educational experience more focused and effective.
